About US

A New Paradigm in the Travel Management Space.

QVC Concierge Limited is a lifestyle management company based in Lagos, Nigeria                      QVC Concierge provides bespoke concierge services to discerning customers including but not limited to travel, music, art, tickets, clubs, spas, restaurants, medical and lifestyle events.  QVC’s unique engagement agency is the voice of authority in luxury lifestyle.                        Proud to partner with the world’s most recognised brands, we combine our unique consumer understanding and channels to transform strategies into reality.

Uncompromising on quality, as a business we are agile problem solvers who are passionately committed to connecting communities and brand partners through authentic, meaningful experiences. 


OUR VISION
To be renowned for World Class travel and concierge services
To be a one stop shop offering all related services with exceptional customer service.
To increase customer accessibility and save resources such as time, effort, energy, and decrease frustrations
To be known for our professionalism, originality, passion and creativity. 

OUR PEOPLE
QVC comprises of people who have over a decade worth of experience in Banking, Aviation, Medical and Customer Services. This makes us dynamic, customer centric, and highly professional individuals whose focus is to deliver unparalleled personalized service to the customer.
We pride ourselves on providing an exceptional customer experience to the discerning client.
Our operations and activities are based on these values
